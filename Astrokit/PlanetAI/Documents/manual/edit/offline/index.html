Welcome to ai.planet!


Welcome to ai.planet, the totally free artificial life simulator! This game is just as entertaining as it is educational, and it even features a full OpenGL 3D engine with plenty of eye candy.

This manual is here to give you a brief overview of ai.planet and the most essential functions and tools it provides. It can't detail every aspect of the game, as ai.planet is a very complex simulation; but you will learn how it works by trying different scenarios. The possibilities are virtually endless. You are the god in ai.planet's world.
